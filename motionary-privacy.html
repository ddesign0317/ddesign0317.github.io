Information We Collect

Motionary stores all data locally on your device only. Streak count, XP, workout history, and settings are stored in your phone's local storage. No personal information is collected or transmitted to any server by Motionary.

Local data is used only to personalise your workout experience within the app. Motionary does not sell or share your data with any third party.
